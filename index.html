About us

We are a group of students from Hochschule Ravensburg-Weingarten (Germany) who decided to start building a robot for University Rover Challenge 2019!
It's a competition taking place in US, Utah, where students from all around the world show their designs. More about the competition: URC 2019

We are grateful to our sponsors:

Do you want to support us? Choose one of the sponsorship packages!

Bronze

Amount: less than 1000 Euros
This package promises our sponsors the right to have their logos presenting on our team official T-shirts and having their advertisements on our social media sites with our recognition for their support.

Silver

Amount: 1000+ Euros
This package promises the same benefits as Bronze package, and also allowing sponsors to have their logos on our final rover which will be competing at the competition. On top of that, we will also include the sponsors' logos on our official brochures.

Golden

Amount: 2000+ Euros
This package promises all the benefits that the Silver package has and more. Sponsors in this package will receive a final report on the project with detailed financial records and their sponsorship will be acknowledged in the report. Moreover, they will also have their logos on our website.

Diamond

Amount: 3000+ Euros
This package encompasses all the privileges that are included in the other three packages, plus the rights for our sponsors to have their logos on our team banner or flag at the competition and also on our project's official posters and flyers.
